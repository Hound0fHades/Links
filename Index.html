About Me

As a true bibliophile, I find immense joy in the written fantasy world—whether through reading or audiobooks. But my passions extend far beyond the literary realm. I’m an avid crafter, a curious explorer, and a passionate seeker of new culinary adventures.

The thrill of travel—both domestically and internationally—fuels my spirit, with Ireland, Scotland, and Greece topping my dream destination list (Greece especially, thanks to my fascination with Greek mythology).

This past year, I’ve delved into the captivating world of cosplay, creating outfits that have been an absolute delight. Whether I’m cooking or chasing new flavors, culinary exploration is its own kind of magic.

Beyond these creative outlets, spending quality time with my loved ones—especially my partner—and friends is what brings me the greatest joy and fulfillment.
