About Me

As a true bibliophile, I find immense joy in the written fantasy world, whether in the form of reading or audiobooks. But my passions extend far beyond the literary realm – I’m an avid crafter, a curious explorer, and a passionate seeker of new culinary adventures. The thrill of travel fuels my spirit, with Ireland, Scotland, and Greece topping my dream list, inspired by a deep fascination with Greek mythology. This past year, I’ve delved into the world of cosplay, creating outfits that have been an absolute delight. Beyond all creativity, spending time with my partner, loved ones, and friends brings me the greatest joy and fulfillment.
